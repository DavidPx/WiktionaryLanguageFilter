Wiktionary Language Filter

There are two modes to choose from: Jumping and Pruning. Jumping will scroll your browser to your desired lanauge. Pruning will remove all content not related to your language(s).

Value is case-sensitive, so if you are interested in Latin enter "Latin".

Use a comma-separated list for your pruning exceptions.

Settings are saved automatically. Reload the page to see the extension do its thing!
